About Royal Spice

A warm glow. A refined table. A story worth savoring.

Since 2012

Tejomay Restaurants brings together a tradition of hospitality and a modern fine-dining sensibility. At Royal Spice, every dish is composed like a scene—layered aromas, balanced spice, and a presentation that feels effortlessly premium.

Our team focuses on detail: marination times, flame-kissed finishes, and sauces crafted for depth. The result is cuisine that feels intimate, cinematic, and genuinely human.
